# Privacy Policy for QuoteBook

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

## Overview

QuoteBook ("the App") is a personal quote management application developed for Android devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect
QuoteBook does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

### Local Data Storage
The App stores the following information locally on your device:
- **Quotes:** Text content and author names you enter
- **Preferences:** App settings such as whether you've completed the onboarding flow

This data is stored using:
- Android Room Database (for quotes)
- Android DataStore (for preferences)

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Collect personal information
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements
- Require account creation or login

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Android's built-in app sandboxing
- Local-only storage with no network transmission
- Standard Android security mechanisms

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:
- Deleting individual quotes within the App
- Clearing the App's data through Android Settings
- Uninstalling the App (removes all associated data)

## Children's Privacy

QuoteBook does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages. The App does not contain any age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
